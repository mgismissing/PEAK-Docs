PEAK - Pico Exploit and Attack Kit
==================================

PEAK is a "simple" tool used by hackers and pentesters to verify the integrity of IT systems.

Buy PEAK devices
----------------
The PEAK device is not sold anywhere: you need to build it yourself. Don't worry, though, you just need to solder and print some pieces together.
Why, you would ask. Well, it's very simple:

"Only those worthy can harness the power of real machinery." [#f1]_
